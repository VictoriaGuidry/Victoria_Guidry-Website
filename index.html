Current college senior studying economics and future marketing specialist or analyst. My passion for economics and human behavior has led me to take several philosophy/psychology courses and major in economics. This year I am still actively studying consumer behavior and I am using any extra time I have to volunteer at my local church and community. Next year I hope to have a job or internship that will help shape, enhance and transform the knowledge I have gained so far into professional skills and experiences.
